Usage of WCAG Guidelines

References:

  1. https://www.w3.org/TR/2006/WD-WCAG20-20060427/guidelines.html
  2. https://getbootstrap.com/docs/4.3/getting-started/accessibility/

Brief Summary
Summary appears at the top of the page and includes jump links to the fields with errors. While each field is listed, no specific information is provided. The inputs themselves identify actual problems.
Detailed Summary
Summary appears at the top of the page and includes jump links to the fields with errors. Each field is listed with specific information.
Brief Statement
A simple statement appears at the top of the page letting the user know there are errors with the form. The message is more generic and actual issues are identified only at the field level.
No Statement
No summary appear, instead focus is given to first field with an error.
